# Google Cybersecurity Certificate – Course 2 Summary  
**Title:** Manage Security Risks

## What I Learned:
- How to assess and prioritize risks using risk = threat × vulnerability × impact.
- The purpose of business continuity planning (BCP) and disaster recovery.
- The NIST Cybersecurity Framework and other models like ISO 27001 and CIS Controls.
- Different control types: administrative, technical, physical.
- How playbooks help guide incident response and align with the incident response lifecycle.

## Key Terms:
- Risk Assessment
- Asset Inventory
- Security Controls
- Security Frameworks (NIST, ISO, CIS)
- Incident Response Playbooks
