## What Is Governance?
- Structure and policies that guide cybersecurity actions
- Defines responsibilities and risk management practices

## Regulations:
- GDPR (Europe): Data protection law for EU citizens
- HIPAA (US): Healthcare data privacy
- PCI-DSS: Credit card industry standard
- CCPA: California consumer privacy

## Frameworks:
- NIST CSF: US cybersecurity framework
- ISO 27001: International information security standard
- CIS Controls: Prioritized security practices

## Compliance:
- Avoids fines, builds trust
- Requires audits, documentation, controls

## Takeaways:
- Regulations = what must be done
- Frameworks = how to do it
