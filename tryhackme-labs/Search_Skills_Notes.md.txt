# TryHackMe – Search Skills

## What Are Search Skills?
Used for passive reconnaissance in penetration testing, threat hunting, or OSINT gathering.

## Google Dorking Operators
- `site:` → search within a domain
- `filetype:` → look for PDFs, .conf, .env
- `intitle:` → search page titles
- `inurl:` → locate admin panels or hidden pages

**Example:**  
site:gov filetype:pdf confidential

## OSINT Tools
- Google  
- Shodan  
- GitHub search (for secrets or public repos)

## Why It Matters
- Every cybersecurity role relies on search skills—from analysts scanning breaches to red teamers doing recon before an attack simulation.
